Ideal Weight Calculator

Reference weight formulas can provide context, but they do not define health on their own. Use them as a broad guide rather than a strict target.

Keep in mind

  • Muscle mass and body frame can change what is realistic for you.
  • Health markers and daily function matter more than one number.
  • Waist size, strength, sleep, and food quality are worth tracking too.

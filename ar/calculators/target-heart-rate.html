Cardio Training

Target Heart Rate Calculator

Heart-rate targets help you plan cardio intensity. They are useful for steady aerobic work, interval sessions, and recovery-focused workouts.

Use with feel

  • Heart rate lags behind effort during very short intervals.
  • Stress, heat, and caffeine can shift readings.
  • Talk test and perceived exertion are still useful.

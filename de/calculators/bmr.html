BMR Calculator

BMR estimates how many calories your body uses at rest. It is useful for understanding your baseline energy needs before activity is added.

Good to know

  • BMR is lower than most people's true maintenance calories.
  • Age, body size, sex, and lean mass all affect this estimate.
  • Use TDEE for a more practical daily calorie target.

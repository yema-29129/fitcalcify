Nutrition Planning

Calorie Deficit Calculator

A calorie deficit is how fat loss happens over time. The key is finding a target that is effective without making hunger, fatigue, and training quality worse than necessary.

Safer fat-loss basics

  • Keep deficits moderate whenever possible.
  • Prioritize protein and resistance training.
  • Track weekly averages, not single days.

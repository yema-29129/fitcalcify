Macro Calculator

Macro targets can make calorie planning more practical by turning an energy target into grams of protein, carbohydrate, and fat.

Best use case

  • Start with calories and protein first.
  • Adjust carbs and fats based on preference and training.
  • Do not let perfect macro tracking replace food quality.

Waist-to-Height Ratio Calculator

Waist-to-height ratio highlights how waist size compares with body height. Many people find it more useful than scale weight alone when tracking abdominal fat.

Why people use it

  • It focuses on waist size, not only body weight.
  • It often changes earlier than the scale during fat-loss phases.
  • It works well alongside BMI and body fat estimates.

Calorie Surplus Calculator

A calorie surplus supports weight gain and muscle-building phases. Smaller surpluses are usually easier to manage and often lead to better body-composition outcomes.

Lean-gain basics

  • Train hard enough to give extra calories a job.
  • Use a modest surplus first.
  • Watch weekly body weight and waist change together.

Sugar Intake Calculator

Added sugar is easy to underestimate. This tool compares your calories with a common guideline and gives a practical target for daily planning.

Common sugar traps

  • Sugary drinks can add up faster than desserts.
  • Sweetened coffee and snacks are easy to forget when logging.
  • Fruit is not the same as added sugar.

Caffeine Intake Calculator

Caffeine can improve alertness and training, but too much or too late in the day can hurt sleep, recovery, and stress levels.

Better caffeine habits

  • Timing affects sleep as much as total amount.
  • Energy drinks and pre-workouts can be stronger than expected.
  • Reducing gradually is easier than stopping suddenly.

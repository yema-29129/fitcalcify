Sodium Intake Calculator

Sodium matters for blood pressure and fluid balance. This page helps you compare your intake with a commonly referenced upper limit.

Where sodium hides

  • Restaurant meals and packaged foods are the biggest sources.
  • Sauces, soups, and processed meats can raise intake quickly.
  • People who sweat heavily may have different needs during training.

VO2 Max Estimate Calculator

VO2 max is a common marker of aerobic fitness. This page gives a rough estimate rather than a lab test result, but it can still be useful for broad training feedback.

Why it matters

  • Better aerobic fitness supports endurance and recovery.
  • Easy cardio done consistently builds the base over time.
  • Lab testing is more accurate than estimate formulas.

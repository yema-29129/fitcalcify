Body Fat Calculator

This tool uses a circumference-based formula for a fast body fat estimate. It is useful for trend tracking but should not be treated as a clinical reading.

How to use it well

  • Measure under similar conditions each time.
  • Track trends over weeks, not day to day.
  • Combine with waist size, progress photos, and training performance.

Water Intake Calculator

Hydration needs change with body size, climate, and training load. This tool gives a practical daily target you can refine using thirst, urine color, and how you feel.

Hydration reminders

  • Hot weather and sweating increase fluid needs.
  • Electrolytes can matter during longer training sessions.
  • Hydration is about steady intake, not catching up all at once.

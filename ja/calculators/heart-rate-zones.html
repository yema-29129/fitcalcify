Cardio Training

Heart Rate Zone Calculator

Training zones help organize easy sessions, threshold work, and harder interval days. Most people do better when easy zones make up the majority of training time.

Training balance

  • Easy zones build aerobic capacity with lower fatigue.
  • Higher zones are effective, but recovery matters.
  • Too much hard work often stalls consistency.

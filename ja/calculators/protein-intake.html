Nutrition Planning

Protein Intake Calculator

Protein helps with satiety, recovery, and muscle retention. A good target depends on body size and whether your goal is general health, fat loss, or muscle gain.

Easy ways to hit the target

  • Distribute protein across several meals.
  • Build meals around a main protein source.
  • Higher-protein breakfasts often make the rest of the day easier.

Sleep and Recovery

Sleep Cycle Calculator

This tool suggests bedtimes by working backwards from your wake-up time using common sleep-cycle timing. It works best when paired with regular habits, not as a perfect rule.

Better sleep basics

  • Keep wake time consistent most days.
  • Reduce bright light and heavy meals late at night.
  • Watch late caffeine if you struggle to fall asleep.

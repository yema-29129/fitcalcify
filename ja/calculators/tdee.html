TDEE Calculator

TDEE estimates how many calories you use in a full day after taking activity into account. It is the best starting point for maintenance, fat loss, or muscle-gain planning.

Practical tip

  • Use this estimate, then compare it with your real weight trend for two to three weeks.
  • Adjust calorie targets slowly when progress is too fast or too slow.
  • Protein, training, sleep, and step count matter with calories.
